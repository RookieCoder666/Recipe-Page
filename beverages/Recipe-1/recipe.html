    Berry Power Smoothie

    This delicious smoothie is packed with antioxidants and vitamins from the berries, making it a perfect refreshing and healthy drink. It's also easily customizable to your taste preferences!

    Recipe Information

    Ingredients :

    • 1 cup mixed berries (frozen or fresh) - strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, blackberries, or a combination of your favorites
    • 1/2 banana (frozen or fresh)
    • 1/2 cup liquid (dairy or non-dairy) - milk (cow's milk, almond milk, oat milk, etc.), yogurt (Greek yogurt, coconut yogurt, etc.) or even water
    • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional) - adjust to your desired sweetness
    • 1 tablespoon nut butter (optional) - peanut butter, almond butter, cashew butter, etc. for added protein and healthy fats

    Cooking Tips and Substitutions :

    • Boost the Protein: Add a scoop of protein powder to your smoothie for an extra protein punch.
    • Add Greens: Sneak in some extra greens by adding a handful of spinach or kale to your smoothie.
    • Make it Vegan: Use non-dairy milk and yogurt options for a vegan smoothie.
    • Sweetener Options: If you don't have honey or maple syrup, you can use another natural sweetener like dates, agave nectar, or stevia.
    • Flavor Variations: Add a squeeze of lemon or lime juice for a tangy twist. You can also experiment with different fruits like mango, pineapple, or peaches.

    Instructions :

    1. Prep: If using fresh berries, wash and pat them dry. If using frozen berries, there's no need to thaw them.
    2. Blend: Add all ingredients to a blender. Blend for 30-60 seconds, or until you reach a smooth and creamy consistency. You may need to stop and scrape down the sides of the blender a couple of times during blending.
    3. Adjust and Enjoy: Taste your smoothie and adjust the sweetness or thickness as desired. Add more liquid for a thinner consistency or more frozen fruit for a thicker smoothie.
