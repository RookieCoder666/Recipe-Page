    Thai Veggie Curry with Coconut Rice

    This fragrant and flavorful curry is packed with fresh vegetables and simmered in a creamy coconut milk sauce. It's a delightful vegetarian twist on a classic Thai dish, perfect for a satisfying and healthy meal.

    Recipe Information

    Prep Time : 15 minutes

    Cook Time : 20 minutes

    Total Time : 35-40 minutes

    Servings : 2-3


    Ingredients :


    for the Curry

    • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
    • 1 tablespoon curry paste (use a green, red, or yellow curry paste according to your preference)
    • 1 (13.5 oz) can coconut milk
    • 1 cup vegetable broth
    • 1 cup chopped bell peppers (any color combination)
    • ½ cup chopped broccoli florets
    • ½ cup chopped green beans
    • ½ cup chopped mushrooms
    • ½ cup cherry tomatoes
    • 1 tablespoon soy sauce (or tamari for a gluten-free option)
    • 1 tablespoon brown sugar (or maple syrup)
    • 1 lime, juiced (about 2 tablespoons)
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Fresh cilantro leaves (optional, for garnish)

    For the Coconut Rice:

    • 1 cup jasmine rice
    • 1 ½ cups water
    • 1 (13.5 oz) can coconut milk
    • ¼ teaspoon salt

    Cooking Tips and Substitutions :

    • Feel free to experiment with different vegetables! Chopped zucchini, snow peas, or baby corn would all be delicious additions.
    • Don't have fresh vegetables? You can use a pre-chopped frozen vegetable mix to save time.
    • For a thicker curry, whisk a tablespoon of cornstarch with a tablespoon of water to create a slurry. Add the slurry to the curry and simmer for an additional minute until thickened.
    • Like it spicier? Add a diced jalapeno pepper or a pinch of red pepper flakes when cooking the curry paste.
    • For a richer curry, add a tablespoon of peanut butter or almond butter to the simmering sauce.

    Instructions :


    Make the Coconut Rice:

    1. In a medium saucepan, combine the jasmine rice, water, coconut milk, and salt.
    2. Bring to a boil over medium heat. Stir once, then reduce heat to low, cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid, and simmer for 15-17 minutes, or until the rice is cooked through and the liquid has been absorbed.
    3. Remove from heat and let the rice sit for 5 minutes before fluffing with a fork.

    Make the Thai Veggie Curry:

    1. While the rice is cooking, heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or pot over medium heat.
    2. Add the curry paste and cook for 30 seconds, stirring constantly, to release the fragrance.
    3. Pour in the coconut milk and vegetable broth. Bring to a simmer.
    4. Add the chopped bell peppers, broccoli florets, green beans, and mushrooms.
    5. Simmer for 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are slightly softened but still crisp-tender.
    6. Add the cherry tomatoes, soy sauce, brown sugar, and lime juice.
    7. Simmer for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the cherry tomatoes soften slightly and the flavors meld.
    8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
