    Mediterranean Chickpea Salad Pitas

    Toss cooked chickpeas with chopped vegetables like cucumber, tomato, red onion, and Kalamata olives. Dress with a lemon vinaigrette and fresh herbs like parsley and mint. Stuff the mixture into whole wheat pitas for a protein-packed and refreshing lunch.

    Recipe Information

    Prep Time : 10 minutes

    Cook Time : Not applicable (using canned chickpeas)

    Total Time : 10 minutes

    Servings : 2-3


    Ingredients :


    For the Chickpea Salad:

    • 1 (15 oz) can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
    • ½ cucumber, diced
    • ½ red onion, diced finely
    • ½ red bell pepper, diced
    • ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
    • 2 tablespoons crumbled vegan feta cheese (or omit for a completely vegan option)
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
    • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
    • Salt and pepper to taste

    For the Pitas:

    • 2 whole wheat pitas
    • Lettuce leaves (optional)

    Cooking Tips and Substitutions :

    • Boost the Protein: Add crumbled tofu or chopped roasted vegetables like zucchini or eggplant for extra protein.
    • Shortcut Option: Use a pre-chopped frozen vegetable mix to save time.
    • Vegan Option: Omit the vegan feta cheese for a completely plant-based dish.
    • Creamy Chickpea Salad: Mash about 1/4 cup of the chickpeas before adding them to the salad mixture for a creamier texture.
    • Added Texture: Include a handful of chopped nuts or Kalamata olives for a bit more crunch.
    • Flavorful Sides: Serve your pitas with chopped tomatoes, sliced avocado, or a dollop of vegan yogurt for extra flavor and creaminess.

    Instructions :

    1. In a large bowl, combine the chickpeas, diced cucumber, red onion, red bell pepper, and chopped parsley.
    2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
    3. Pour the dressing over the chickpea and vegetable mixture and toss to coat everything evenly.
    4. Taste and adjust seasonings with additional salt and pepper if needed.
    5. If using lettuce leaves, line the bottom half of each pita bread with a leaf.
    6. Fill the pita with the flavorful chickpea salad.
