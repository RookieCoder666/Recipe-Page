    Scrambled Tofu with Veggies

    This protein-packed and flavorful pure veg scrambled tofu is a delicious and healthy vegetarian breakfast option. It's easy to customize with your favorite vegetables and spices, making it a versatile and satisfying way to start your day.

    Recipe Information

    Prep Time : 5 minutes

    Cook Time : 10-12 minutes

    Total Time : 15-17 minutes

    Servings : 1-2

    Nutritional Information (per serving) :

    • Calories : Around 250-300 (depending on the vegetables and oil used)
    • Protein : Around 15-20g from the tofu
    • Carbs : Around 10-15g from the vegetables
    • Fat : Around 5-10g from the oil and nutritional yeast (if using)

    Ingredients :

    • 1 block (14 oz) extra-firm tofu, drained and pressed
    • ½ tablespoon olive oil
    • ½ cup chopped onion
    • ½ cup chopped bell pepper (any color combination)
    • ¼ cup chopped mushrooms
    • ½ cup chopped spinach (or other leafy greens like kale)
    • 1 tablespoon nutritional yeast (optional, for a cheesy flavor)
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • ¼ teaspoon turmeric powder (optional, for a yellow color)
    • Chopped fresh herbs like cilantro or parsley (optional, for garnish)

    Cooking Tips and Substitutions :

    • Pressing the tofu removes excess moisture, which helps it crumble more easily and brown better. You can press the tofu by placing it between two cutting boards with a heavy weight on top for 15-20 minutes.
    • Feel free to experiment with different vegetables! Chopped broccoli florets, zucchini, or cherry tomatoes would all be delicious additions.
    • Don't have fresh vegetables? You can use a pre-chopped frozen vegetable mix to save time.
    • To crumble the tofu easily, use a fork or a potato masher.
    • Want to add a smoky flavor? Add a pinch of smoked paprika to the pan with the vegetables.
    • For a kick of spice, add a diced jalapeno pepper or a pinch of red pepper flakes.
    • Make it gluten-free! Use gluten-free tamari or soy sauce instead of regular soy sauce (if using).
    • Serve your scrambled tofu with toast, a whole wheat tortilla, or a bed of greens for a more complete breakfast.

    Instructions :

    1. Crumble the drained and pressed tofu with your hands or a fork.
    2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet or pan over medium heat.
    3. Add the chopped onion and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until softened.
    4. Add the chopped bell pepper, mushrooms, and any other vegetables you are using. Cook for an additional 3-5 minutes, or until softened.
    5. Add the crumbled tofu to the pan with the vegetables.
    6. Sprinkle with turmeric powder (if using) and season with salt and pepper to taste.
    7. Using a spatula, gently scramble the tofu mixture, breaking up any large chunks, until heated through.
    8. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the tofu is slightly browned.
    9. Stir in the nutritional yeast (if using) and cook for another minute.
    10. Remove from the heat and garnish with chopped fresh herbs like cilantro or parsley (optional).
