    Thai Tom Yum Soup with Tofu

    Simmer a fragrant tom yum soup base with lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, galangal, and chilies in vegetable broth. Add vegetables like mushrooms, straw mushrooms, and cherry tomatoes. Submerge cubes of pan-fried tofu for a protein addition. Finish with a squeeze of lime juice and fresh cilantro for a vibrant and flavorful vegetarian soup.

    Recipe Information

    Prep Time : 10 minutes

    Cook Time : 30 minutes

    Total Time : 40 minutes

    Servings : 2-3


    Ingredients :


    For the Broth:

    • 4 cups vegetable broth
    • 2 stalks lemongrass, bruised (white and light green parts only)
    • 2 kaffir lime leaves, torn
    • 2-3 slices galangal (optional, can substitute with ginger)
    • 1-inch piece ginger, peeled and thinly sliced
    • 1-2 dried red chilies (depending on your spice preference)
    • 1 tablespoon palm sugar (or brown sugar)
    • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
    • 1 teaspoon lime juice

    For the Soup:

    • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
    • 1 cup chopped onion
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 (14 oz) block firm tofu, drained and cubed
    • 1-2 red bell peppers, sliced (optional)
    • 1 cup chopped mushrooms (cremini, shiitake, or button mushrooms all work well)
    • Â½ cup cherry tomatoes
    • 1 (13.5 oz) can coconut milk
    • Fresh cilantro leaves (for garnish)
    • Cooked rice or noodles (optional, for serving)

    Cooking Tips and Substitutions :

    • For a thicker soup, whisk a tablespoon of cornstarch with a tablespoon of water to create a slurry. Add the slurry to the simmering soup and cook for an additional minute until thickened.
    • Tom Yum paste can be used to add extra flavor to the broth. Add 1-2 tablespoons of Tom Yum paste along with the other broth ingredients and simmer for 15 minutes.
    • Don't have kaffir lime leaves? You can substitute with the zest of 1 lime.
    • Like it spicier? Add a few more slices of red chili pepper or a pinch of red pepper flakes to the broth while simmering.
    • Feel free to experiment with other vegetables like broccoli florets, baby corn, or green beans.

    Instructions :


    Prepare the Broth Ingredients :

    1. Bruise the lemongrass stalks by gently whacking them with the back of a spoon.
    2. Tear the kaffir lime leaves into small pieces.
    3. Peel and thinly slice the ginger.
    4. Drain and cube the firm tofu.
    5. Chop the onion and garlic.
    6. If using red bell peppers, slice them.
    7. Wash and halve the cherry tomatoes.
    8. In a large pot, combine the vegetable broth, lemongrass stalks, kaffir lime leaves, galangal (or ginger), sliced ginger, red chilies, palm sugar, soy sauce, and lime juice.
    9. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes.

    Prepare the Soup:

    1. While the broth simmers, heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or pan over medium heat.
    2. Add the chopped onion and cook for 3-5 minutes, or until softened.
    3. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional 30 seconds.
    4. Add the cubed tofu to the pan and cook for a few minutes on each side, until lightly golden brown.
    5. You can also add sliced red bell peppers at this point, if using.
    6. Strain the flavorful broth into the pot, discarding the lemongrass stalks, kaffir lime leaves, galangal (or ginger), and red chilies.
    7. Add the cooked tofu, vegetables from the pan, chopped mushrooms, and cherry tomatoes to the broth.
    8. Bring the soup to a simmer and cook for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and the tofu is heated through.
    9. Season with additional soy sauce or lime juice to taste, if desired.
