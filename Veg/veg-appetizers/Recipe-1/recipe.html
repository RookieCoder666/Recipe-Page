    Spiced Chickpea Fritters

    These crispy and flavorful fritters are a delicious and healthy vegetarian option, perfect for a snack, appetizer, or even a light main course. Packed with protein from the chickpeas and customizable with your favorite spices, they're a satisfying and easy-to-make crowd-pleaser.

    Recipe Information

    Prep Time : 10 minutes

    Cook Time : 5-7 minutes per side

    Total Time : 15-17 minutes

    Servings : 4-6 fritters

    Nutritional Information (per serving, about 2 fritters) :

    • Calories : Around 200-250 (depending on the oil used)
    • Protein : Around 5-7g from the chickpeas
    • Carbs : Around 20-30g from the chickpeas and vegetables
    • Fat : Around 5-10g from the oil

    Ingredients :

    • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, rinsed and drained
    • ½ cup chopped onion
    • 1 small green chili pepper, chopped (optional, for spice)
    • 1 clove garlic, minced
    • ½ cup chopped fresh cilantro
    • ½ cup chopped fresh parsley
    • 1 tablespoon gram flour (also known as besan flour)
    • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
    • ½ teaspoon ground cumin
    • ½ teaspoon ground coriander
    • ¼ teaspoon turmeric powder
    • Pinch of red pepper flakes (optional)
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Vegetable oil for frying

    Cooking Tips and Substitutions :

    • Don't have gram flour? You can substitute it with all-purpose flour, but the fritters may not be as crispy.
    • For a smoother texture, mash some of the chickpeas before adding them to the mixture.
    • Like a bit more moisture? Add a tablespoon of chopped fresh mint or chopped vegetables like zucchini or carrots to the batter.
    • Short on time? You can use pre-chopped frozen onions and peppers to save time.
    • For a vegan option, ensure your gram flour or all-purpose flour is certified vegan.

    Instructions :

    1. In a large bowl, mash half of the chickpeas with a fork. You can leave the other half roughly chopped.
    2. Add the chopped onion, green chili pepper (if using), garlic, chopped cilantro, chopped parsley, gram flour, cornstarch, ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric powder, red pepper flakes (if using), and salt and pepper to taste.
    3. Mix well to combine all the ingredients. The mixture should be thick and slightly sticky. If it's too dry, add a tablespoon of water at a time until it reaches the desired consistency.
    4. Heat enough vegetable oil in a large skillet or pan over medium heat. The oil should be about ½ inch deep.
    5. Once the oil is hot, scoop out about 2 tablespoons of the chickpea mixture and form it into a patty. Gently place the patty in the hot oil.
    6. Fry the fritters for 2-3 minutes per side, or until golden brown and crispy.
    7. Drain the cooked fritters on paper towels to remove excess oil.
    8. Repeat steps 5-7 with the remaining chickpea mixture.
    9. Serve your Spiced Chickpea Fritters immediately while hot. They are delicious on their own or with a side of chutney, yogurt dip, or chopped vegetables.
